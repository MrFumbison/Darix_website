DarIX

DARIX is an Internet Exchange Point (IXP) in Dar es Salaam Tanzania. DARIX was started as a result of huge demand of keeping local traffic local and enhance user experience. Organizations are invited to connect to DARIX in order to have access to direct routes from many diverse peering partners
